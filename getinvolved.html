Volunteer

The café is staffed entirely by volunteers.

Come to one of our volunteer orientations to learn more and start contributing. We hold orientations the first Friday of every month at 7 PM and the second Saturday of every month at 10 AM.

Foster

Want a companion but not ready to commit?
Travel a lot?
Just want to test the puppy waters?

Fostering is for you. You can house a dog for just a week or for several months. Applications are available at the café.
