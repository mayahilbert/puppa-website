Puppa is a non-profit dog café.

We opened on December 1st, 2017 to share the love of dogs, animal rescue, and teatime. Whether you are a tea-person or a coffee-person, we can all unite on the value of quality time with a treat and a dog.

Get to know your new best friends over a beverage of your choice from our menu.

All dogs at the café are neutered, vaccinated, and adoptable. All edibles served are vegan and locally sourced. All proceeds go toward animal rescue.
